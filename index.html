Unfolding Maps:
(de)constructing contemporary geospatial technologies

In this two-part workshop we will examine and deploy contemporary technologies for the analysis and display of geo-spatial data, underlying much of the hype around urban "smart" technologies. Informed by critical thinking on code and space we will first address established methods of cartographic representation to then move towards an "hands-on" exploration of the tools and techniques of modern, digital mapping practice.
